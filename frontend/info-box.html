Welcome to Regnum Nostalgia!
Many features are playable, but the core combat loop is still in development.
What works:
  • Right-click → "Walk here" (Pathfinding)
  • Health/Mana Stats and Regeneration
  • Character Attributes and Stats
  • Inventory and Equipment (10 slots)
  • Quickbar (4 rows × 10 slots, keys 1–0)
  • Consumable Spells (potions via inventory or quickbar)
  • Map with Regions and walkable terrain
  • Territories and World Bosses (live status)
  • See other players in real-time
  • Chat / Shoutbox
  • Path Builder and Screenshot Manager
Not yet available:
  • Combat (attacking territories and bosses)
  • Quests and NPCs
  • Crafting, Gathering, and Collectables
  • Party and Guild system
Warning: The database may be wiped with any update. Expect loss of characters, inventory, and world state.
